OrbitKit is a simple personal tools app for everyday planning and tracking.

Use OrbitKit to estimate compound interest growth, calculate mortgage payments, and track active fasting sessions without clutter or complicated setup.

Features:
- Compound interest calculator with visual growth breakdown
- Mortgage calculator with payment and amortization visuals
- Fasting tracker that shows elapsed fasting time
- Fasting milestones and achievements
- Clean iPhone and iPad interface
- Local-first experience with no account required

OrbitKit is designed for quick checks, personal planning, and lightweight daily tracking.
